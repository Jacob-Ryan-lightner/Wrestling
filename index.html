Types of wrestling

  • Freestyle: Allows attacks on upper and lower body, one of 2 wrestling styles
  • Folkstyle: The most common for highschool wrestling, focusing on control and pins
  • Grecco-Roman: resticts attacks to only below waist to focus on throws against opponents

Rules of Wrestling

  • Each match has 3 periods with a pin ending a match
  • Score points on takedowns, stand ups, and reversals
  • Matches are instantly won when oppenent is pinned (both shoulders on mat for 3 seconds) or a technical fall(15 points over opponents score)
